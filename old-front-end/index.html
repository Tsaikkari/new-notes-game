How to play

Levels: The note goes to its place on the staff. Your job is to memorize its place and then click on the key on your keyboard (not the piano keyboard key on the screen) corresponding to the name of note. Put the sound on (loud)!

Tests: When you have finished memorizing the staff positions of the notes you can test if you've learned them properly. Click the keyboard key on the screen corresponding to the note on the staff.
