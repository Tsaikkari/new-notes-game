How to play

Levels: Click the key on your keyboard (not the piano keyboard key on the screen) corresponding to the name of the key that the note is approaching. If you hit the right key the note will go to its place on the staff. Put the sound on (loud)!

Tests: Click the keyboard key on the screen corresponding to the note on the staff.

Choose a level and press any key to start
